## Project Overview

This project presents a complete Business Intelligence solution for analyzing Madhav Store’s online sales performance across India. Using Power BI for visualization and Excel datasets for analysis, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights related to customers, states, categories, payment methods, profits, and sales trends.

The dashboard helps track business performance, identify profitable segments, understand customer purchasing behavior, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

**Technologies Used:** Power BI · Excel · DAX · Data Visualization · Business Intelligence

---

# Objective

The owner of Madhav Store wanted an interactive dashboard to track and analyze online sales performance across India. The main objective of this project was to convert raw sales data into actionable insights that help monitor revenue, profit, customer behavior, category performance, and regional sales trends efficiently.

# Key Insights

### ⭐ Category Performance

* Clothing category recorded the highest number of sales transactions.
* Electronics generated strong profit contribution.
* Furniture category showed moderate sales performance.

### ⭐ Payment Analysis

* COD (Cash on Delivery) was the most preferred payment method.
* UPI and Debit Card payments also contributed significantly.

### ⭐ Regional Analysis

* Sales were distributed across multiple Indian states.
* Maharashtra, Delhi, and Uttar Pradesh showed strong customer activity.

### ⭐ Business Performance

* Certain sub-categories generated higher profitability despite lower quantities.
* Interactive filters helped identify high-performing products and regions quickly.
